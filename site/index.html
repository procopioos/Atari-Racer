About the Game

ATARI RACER is an arcade-style racing game built with Python and Pygame. Steer through four lanes of traffic, collect coins, chain combos for score multipliers, and unlock achievements as you push for a new high score.

Features

Car Skins

10 different skins with unique speed bonuses.

Boost System

Press SPACE to activate a speed boost, costs 50 points.

Combo Multiplier

Chain obstacles, near misses, and coins for multipliers up to x4.

Achievements

Unlock milestones as you play, tracked across every run.

Particle Effects

Explosions, sparks, and boost trail effects.

Sound Effects

Procedurally generated audio for coins, boost, hits, and more on desktop.

Difficulty Levels

Easy, Medium, and Hard, each with its own pace and obstacle density.

Garage Upgrades

Spend coins on permanent speed and life upgrades.
